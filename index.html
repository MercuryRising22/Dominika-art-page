Welcome to wonderland of Dominika. Fragile soul expressed by painting art, coming from the bottom of the heart.

I am inspired

I see the world as the source of energy together with the universe. I see nature and people as atoms, with certain amount of energy. My first collection was about inside energy. As I believe that this is possible to absorb energy and use it for the right creative purpose. I find colours important in my art, because every colour has different absorbsion and emition of energy. Each colour is equally important in nature.

If something matters, it is probably what we put to the world, how far we commit ourselves to realize ideas in order to make the change for better. I do believe that the world might be a place of peace as only we try to focus our attention and skills on right projects. The shortness of life, which I experienced personally, showed me that we should never wait for another chance to put enough of our effort. Those given chances are unique and fragile, so easy to be stolen by heartless time.

Dominika Kłosińska
